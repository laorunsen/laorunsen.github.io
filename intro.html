公司简介

中山双创照明灯饰厂成立于2009年，其前身为中山比珥灯饰厂，于2016年优化重组成为中山双创照明灯饰厂。双创照明是一家集产品设计、生产、销售以及售后服务为一体的专业化照明技术公司，主要产品有：LED筒灯、LED轨道射灯、LED格栅灯、LED壁灯等商业照明产品。我们专业的LED产品已经大量应用在全国各地城市，并远销欧美、东南亚等地区。

我们以打造全球商业照明第一品牌为愿景，以为全球各种商业店铺及展厅创造最佳商业光环境为使命，双创人将众志成城，持之以恒，不断“创造、创新”。

我们为客户而来，双创人将自始至终地追求客户高度满意，“最满意的服务”是我们每一个双创人首要工作标准! 双创人将一如既往以诚信、创新、勤劳、务实的作风，致力于为社会缔造绿色节能、最佳艺术的商业光环境，创造良好的社会效益，推动照明产业健康快速向前发展。

Company profile

Zhongshan CI Lighting Factory, whose predecessor was Peer Lighting Factory, was established in 2009.In 2016, it was optimized to reorganize to become Zhongshan CI Lighting Factory. CI Lighting is a professional lighting technology company which engages in product design, production, sales and after-sales service. Our main products are, LED tube light, LED track lamp, LED grille lamp, LED wall lamp and other commercial lighting. Our professional LED products have been widely used in cities throughout the countries and have been exported to Europe, America and southeast Asia, etc.

Our goal is to create the first brand in global commercial lighting, putting the creation of the best lighting environment for various commercial shops and showrooms worldwide as our mission. CI Lighting will overcome various difficulties and persevere to constant creation and innovation.

We work for our customers. We are seeking for the satisfaction from our customers all the time. "The most satisfying service" is the priority for each of us. We will take integrity, innovation, hardworking and pragmatic as working style. We work hard to create green energy saving and the best art commercial lighting environment for the society. And we endeavor to create a better social benefits as well as promoting the lighting industry to develop healthily and rapidly.
